Reborn as a Vending Machine, I Now Wander the Dungeon

A vending machine fanatic meets his demise when he is, quite ironically, crushed by a vending machine. He finds himself reincarnated as a vending machine in another world. Now he must navigate a strange and dangerous dungeon with his new form while trying to find out how to survive in this bizarre reincarnation.
